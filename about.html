About Me

Designer, Coder, Outdoor

Hello! My name is Yiling. I am currently a master's student in the School of Information at the University of Texas at Austin, and I am always interested in learning how technology can be used to benefit society.

I volunteered with an Indian NGO to help them create a content management system. I also worked for a Taiwanese traditional soy sauce manufacturer, where I led eco-friendly product development and implemented brewing process management systems. During these experiences, I realized the importance of human-centered design, regardless of the technology used.

I care about the user experience, and I also enjoy writing code to create things. I enjoy learning and hope to continue learning to develop technology that will benefit society.
